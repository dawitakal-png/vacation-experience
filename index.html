Picture Perfect Trip: Vacation in Amsterdam

Where would you go?

If I could take a vacation, I would travel to Amsterdam, Netherlands during the summer. The city is known for its historic canals, colorful houses, and rich cultural history. I would visit the Anne Frank House, the Van Gogh Museum, and the Rijksmuseum to experience world-class art and history. Walking and biking along the canal belt would allow me to see the city from a local’s perspective (Iamsterdam, n.d.).

What would you do?

During my vacation, I would enjoy activities that combine relaxation and adventure. I would explore the city by water, bike through historic neighborhoods, and walk through local markets to experience everyday life in Amsterdam (Iamsterdam, n.d.).

Figure 2. Canal cruise in Amsterdam. Reprinted from Canalsofamsterdam.com (2025).

  • Take a canal cruise through the city
  • Ride a bike along Amsterdam’s streets
  • Visit Albert Cuyp Market for shopping
  • Explore parks and historic neighborhoods

What would you eat?

Sampling local cuisine would be one of the highlights of my trip. I would enjoy poffertjes, which are small fluffy pancakes, and stroopwafels filled with sweet caramel. I would also try Dutch cheese, herring, and other popular street foods while exploring the city (DutchAmsterdam, 2023).
